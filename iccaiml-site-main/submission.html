Submission Guidelines

How to submit your paper to ICCAIML

Instructions for Authors

  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • Papers should be written in English and formatted according to the conference template.
  • Maximum length: 8 pages (including references).
  • Submit your paper in PDF format via the conference submission portal.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present at the conference.
